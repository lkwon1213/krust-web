Please take a moment to carefully read through our privacy policy.

Collected information

In order to continue to improve our services, Orca Games use third-party SDKs in our services which may collect personal information. This personal information includes but is not limited to advertising identifiers (Google advertiser ID and Apple IDFA) and IP addresses. Please take a look at the privacy policies listed in the ‘Third-Party Operations’ section below for more information regarding the types of data these services will collect.

All information collected by Orca Games or our third party services will only be used for limited purposes permitted by applicable laws.

Targeted Advertising

Our advertising partners use personal information collected from our users in order to measure effectiveness of their advertising campaigns, user’s interests, and user’s reactions to certain advertisements. If you are not interested in such targeted advertisements, you can set settings in your mobile device so that this is disabled. For iOS you can do so under Settings > Privacy > Advertising. For Android, you can find it under Settings > Google > Ads > Opt out of Ads Personalization.
